Carbonara

Indulge in the classic and comforting flavors of Carbonara pasta. This Italian dish features spaghetti or other pasta varieties coated in a creamy sauce made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta or bacon, and black pepper. The result is a rich and velvety texture with a perfect balance of salty and savory flavors. Every bite of Carbonara pasta is a satisfying and decadent experience, making it a favorite choice for pasta lovers.

Mac and Cheese

Delight in the creamy and cheesy goodness of Mac and Cheese. This beloved dish combines elbow macaroni pasta with a luscious cheese sauce, typically made with cheddar or a combination of cheeses. The result is a comforting and indulgent dish with a smooth and gooey texture that melts in your mouth. Mostacciol

Mostacciol

Discover the tastes of Mostaccioli pasta, a tube-shaped pasta with a slightly greater diameter than penne. This versatile pasta goes great with a variety of sauces, including classic marinara, creamy Alfredo, and strong meat-based sauces. Mostaccioli's firm structure wonderfully holds the sauce, offering a pleasant blend of flavors and textures in every bite.

Spaghetti
